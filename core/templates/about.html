About B.Tech-Buddy

A student-first learning platform built with passion

Our Mission

B.Tech-Buddy is designed to make academic resources easily accessible to students — including notes, faculty material, PYQs, and curated learning tutorials — all in one organized platform.

Our goal is to reduce confusion, save time, and help students learn smarter, not harder.
